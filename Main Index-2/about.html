Our Company

Who We Are

SSAP Tech Solutions LLP is a dynamic IT services and consulting firm registered by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), Government of India. Since our inception in January 2022, we have been delivering cutting-edge technology solutions to businesses across various industries.

Our team of passionate professionals combines technical expertise with business acumen to deliver solutions that drive digital transformation and operational excellence.

Headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, we serve clients across India with plans for global expansion in the coming years.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted technology partner

Company Founded

January 2022

SSAP Tech Solutions LLP was officially registered with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India. Our journey began with a small team of 5 professionals and our first office in Pune.

First Major Client

June 2022

Secured our first enterprise client, marking our entry into the corporate IT services market. This project involved developing a custom CRM solution for a mid-sized manufacturing company.

ISO Certification

March 2023

Achieved ISO 27001:2013 certification for our information security management systems, demonstrating our commitment to data security and best practices.

Team Expansion

September 2023

Expanded our team to 25 professionals and moved to a larger office space in Kharadi, Pune to accommodate our growing business and service offerings.

Current Milestones

2024

Serving over 50 clients across various industries with a team of 40+ professionals. Expanding our service portfolio to include advanced cybersecurity solutions and AI-driven applications.
